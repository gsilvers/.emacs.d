* Until Loop

  This comes from an article https://emacstil.com/til/2021/12/19/my-first-macro-until-loop/

  The user deines an untill loop

  #+begin_src emacs-lisp

    (defmacro until (test &rest body)
      `(while (not ,test)
	 ,@body))

    (setq jz/counter 1)
    (setq jz/res '())
    (until (>= jz/counter 5)
	   (setq jz/counter (+ 1 jz/counter))
	   (setq jz/res (cons jz/counter jz/res)))

    ;; jz/res => (5 4 3 2)
  #+end_src
